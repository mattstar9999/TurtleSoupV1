Strategy

Mark off the high and the low of the previous 1-hour candle

On 1 min chart, if the candle closes above the previous 1-hour candle high, place a long trade

On 1 min chart, if the candle closes below the previous 1-hour candle low, place a short trade

On 1 min chart, if the candle crosses but doesnt close above the previous 1-hour candle high, place a short trade

On 1 min chart, if the candle crosses but doesnt close below the previous 1-hour candle low, place a long trade

Take profit should be a tick variable that the user can input
Stop loss is a tick variable as well
